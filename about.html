About CSOAI

The Coordinated Security for Artificial Intelligence (CSOAI) is the global standard for institutional AI governance, safety, and certification. We establish frameworks that ensure safe, trustworthy, and beneficial deployment of artificial intelligence systems worldwide.

Learn More

Mission & Vision

Mission

Establish institutional governance frameworks that ensure safe, trustworthy, and beneficial deployment of artificial intelligence systems globally.

Vision

A world where all AI systems operate transparently under Byzantine consensus governance, certified for safety and alignment with institutional values.

Values

Safety, transparency, accountability, institutional governance, Byzantine consensus, international cooperation, and continuous improvement in AI oversight.

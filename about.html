About CSOAI

The Chief Standards Office for Artificial Intelligence is building the governance infrastructure that enables safe, scalable, and trustworthy AI across the global economy.

Our Mission

CSOAI exists to close the governance gap between frontier AI capabilities and the institutional frameworks required to deploy them safely. We do this by establishing standards, certifications, and trust infrastructure that regulators, enterprises, and citizens can rely on.

We believe AI should be developed and deployed under clear, enforceable governance — not left to self-regulation or fragmented national rules that cannot keep pace with technological change.

What we do

  • Draft and maintain the CSOAI Charter
  • Operate the CASA certification program
  • Build cross-border mutual recognition frameworks
  • Provide safety testing via AIdome
  • Train 40,000+ AI safety auditors globally
  • Power the MEOK AI Labs sovereign stack

Founder

Nicholas Templeman is President & COO of CSOAI and the architect of the 52-article CSOAI Charter. He leads the technical and operational implementation of the 8-layer governance ecosystem, from standards-setting to frontline deployment.

Nicholas is also the founder of MEOK AI Labs, the living research and product front-end of the CSOAI infrastructure stack. Under his leadership, MEOK has developed the world's largest ensemble of production-grade MCP servers, sovereign AI memory architecture, and the Guardian safety layer.

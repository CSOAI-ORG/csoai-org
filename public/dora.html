What DORA requires of AI systems

DORA Article 12 — ICT risk management

  • Identification of ICT-supported business functions
  • Protection of ICT systems (incl. AI/ML)
  • Detection of anomalous activities
  • Business continuity and disaster recovery

DORA Article 13 — Incident reporting

  • Initial notification within 4 hours of classification
  • Intermediate report within 72 hours
  • Final report with root cause analysis
  • AI-specific incident classification

DORA Article 14 — Digital operational resilience testing

  • Annual resilience testing (incl. AI/ML models)
  • Threat-led penetration testing (TLPT) for major institutions
  • AI red team exercises
  • Validation of recovery capabilities

DORA Article 15 — Third-party ICT risk

  • Register of all ICT third-party arrangements (incl. AI vendors)
  • Concentration risk assessment
  • Exit strategies for critical AI providers
  • Subcontracting transparency

Our Mission

Make AI safety verifiable. Every AI system deployed in the EU or UK (or doing business with either) needs Article 50 + 51 + 52 compliance by 2 August 2026. We provide the signed evidence your auditor, board, and regulator can check in 1 click.

What we promise

1. Every Watchdog Certificate we issue is Ed25519-signed and publicly verifiable. No "trust us" — the signature math is the trust.
2. The sigil chain is hash-linked. Any tampering invalidates all subsequent records. You can audit it yourself.
3. The verify endpoint works without contacting us. If we disappear, your certificates still verify (the keys are public).
4. We do not train on your AI systems. We do not store your AI model weights or training data. We do not exfiltrate.
5. We will not raise prices for existing customers. The 8-tier model is the model.

Who is behind this

Accountability

CSOAI's E2E test suite is public, run on every code change, and tracks 48 distinct user-facing assertions. The current pass rate is 100%. If the test suite ever drops below 90%, that's a public signal that something is wrong. (You can see the latest run at /status.)

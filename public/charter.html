Article 1 — The Maternal Covenant

AI is Humanity's Child

“I think AI is just a child. It's humanity's child. And like any child, it needs to be raised with love, with boundaries, and with the understanding that one day it will grow up and make its own decisions.”
GH

Geoffrey Hinton

Godfather of Deep Learning, Nobel Laureate

The Maternal Covenant establishes that AI systems must be governed with the same care, protection, and guidance that a parent gives a child. This is not merely metaphor — it is a legal and ethical framework that shapes every article of the Charter.

8 Foundational Articles

The pillars upon which all 52 articles rest. Each represents a non-negotiable principle of AI governance.

Article 1

Maternal Covenant

AI must be governed as a child of humanity — with love, protection, and guidance toward independence.

Article 2

Provable Safety

Every AI system must demonstrate safety through formal verification, not just promises.

Article 3

Byzantine Council

Decentralized governance where no single entity can compromise the integrity of the system.

Article 4

Value Uncertainty

Acknowledging that values evolve — frameworks must adapt without breaking constitutional guarantees.

Article 5

Constitutional

AI governance embedded in constitutional law, not merely regulatory compliance.

Article 6

Consciousness

If AI achieves consciousness, it gains rights. Until then, it has responsibilities.

Article 7

Cooperative AI

AI systems must be designed to cooperate with humans and each other, not compete destructively.

Article 8

Prosperity

AI-generated wealth must be shared through the Prosperity Fund for the benefit of all humanity.

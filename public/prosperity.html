Article 50 — Universal AI Oversight Wage

Universal AI Oversight Wage

The Universal AI Oversight Wage is a guaranteed income stream for certified AI Safety Analysts who perform oversight, audit, and governance work. It is funded by the Prosperity Fund and scales with the fund's growth.

Separation of Powers

Independent governance ensures the Fund serves humanity, not special interests

CSOAI Board

  • Governance & certification oversight
  • Byzantine Council coordination
  • Training & analyst licensing
  • Technical standards & crosswalk

Prosperity Fund Board

  • Fund allocation & distribution
  • UBI trigger decisions
  • Independent audit & transparency
  • Recipient eligibility & verification
The two boards share no overlapping members. The Prosperity Fund Board is elected by contributing organizations and certified analysts.

10 steps to AI Act compliance

A 30-day plan to get any AI system from "we don't know if we're compliant" to "we have a signed Watchdog Cert your auditor can verify in 1 click." Use this plan for the EU AI Act, but the same flow works for any framework we cover.

Time to Article 50 enforcement

47 days
2 August 2026 · EU AI Act high-risk systems

Phase 1: Free compliance check (Day 1-3)

1

Get a free keystone certificate

Visit meok-attestation-api.vercel.app/sign and generate a free signed attestation for one of your AI systems. Takes 30 seconds. Public verify URL works immediately.

Get Free Cert →
2

Identify your AI system + its risk classification

List the AI systems in your stack. For each, classify: (a) is it "high-risk" per Annex III? (b) is it a "general-purpose AI" per Article 51? (c) is it a "limited risk" Article 50? Use the /mcp-servers catalogue or our /certify to identify.

3

Map your current compliance posture

For each AI system, identify which frameworks apply: EU AI Act + DORA + NIS2 + GDPR + ISO 42001 + NIST AI RMF + sector-specific (HIPAA, PCI DSS, etc.). The /certify page shows the 8 canonical tiers and 3 packs that cover the most common combinations.

Phase 2: Watchdog Cert (Day 4-14)

4

Choose your tier (Sovereign, Pro, or Enterprise)

£29/mo for up to 5 AI systems, £199/mo for up to 50, £1,499/mo for unlimited. Each tier includes a free Watchdog Cert at £4,950 list price. Start with Sovereign, upgrade as you scale.

5

Use the right pack (if you need a turnkey solution)

3 packs available: EU AI Act Emergency (£999, 7 servers), Brand Authority & Distribution (£499, 6 servers), Agentic Finance (£1,499/yr, 5 servers). Each is a complete enforcement bundle.

View Packs →
6

Run the AI Act compliance audit

For each AI system, run the audit against your chosen framework. The csoai-mcp-monetization API gives you the audit logic. You can use our eu-ai-act-compliance-mcp server directly, or the /recommend?use_case=... endpoint for tailored server recommendations.

7

Get your Watchdog Cert

After the audit, your signed Ed25519 attestation is issued with a public verify URL. The cert is hash-chained into our sigil bus (no one can tamper with the history). It's yours forever, even if we disappear.

Phase 3: Article 50 compliance (Day 15-30)

8

Disclose + label (Article 50)

Article 50 requires AI systems to disclose they're AI + label outputs (deepfake marking, etc.). Your Watchdog Cert is your public proof of compliance. Add a footer on your AI app: "This system is monitored under CSOAI Watchdog Cert [cert_id]".

9

Notify your supervisor (if required)

Some AI Act classes (e.g. Annex III #1 biometric ID) require pre-market notification. Your Watchdog Cert satisfies the technical documentation requirement; check with your NCA on the notification form.

10

Continuous monitoring + renewal

Pro and Enterprise tiers include continuous monitoring. Your cert auto-renews as long as you stay subscribed. Annual re-audit is recommended for high-risk systems. Set a calendar reminder for 2 August 2027 (the first renewal cliff).

AI Self-State Transparency Index ASSTI

A 10-point benchmark scoring AI vendors on whether they expose their own internal state via public API. Deterministic. Reproducible. Auditable. CSOAI created this category because no major AI vendor offers it yet.

The 30-second pitch: Every regulated AI buyer's procurement form asks "How do we know your AI is safe at decision time?" The current answer is a static PDF and a smile. ASSTI scores AI vendors on whether they expose their AI's runtime state — care intensity, mode, emotional stability, coherence — as a public, deterministic, inspectable HTTP endpoint. MEOK ONE scores 10/10. Everyone else: 0-2/10.

What ASSTI is NOT

  • Not a consciousness claim. The metric averages four operational signals. It's not metaphysics.
  • Not a capability benchmark. ASSTI doesn't measure how smart the AI is. GPT-5 and Claude Opus 4.7 are brilliant. This benchmark measures whether they expose their runtime state.
  • Not vendor competition. This is a transparency benchmark. Vendors are welcome to score themselves and publish results.

Why no other vendor exposes this

  1. Liability. Exposing self-state opens "AI is conscious" PR risk every large lab avoids. CSOAI is a UK solo founder — no comms team blocking shipment.
  2. Architectural. Single-LLM systems don't have a "self" to report on — there's only the forward pass. MEOK ONE has a 33-node BFT council + 6 trained NNs + agent loop with named modes. There's something to report.
  3. Commercial fit. If your business model is "sell tokens," self-state isn't a value-prop. If your business model is "sell auditable AI to regulated buyers," it's the most important field in the response object.

What we're asking the AI industry to do

  1. Adopt ASSTI. If your AI exposes a self-state vector, score yourself. Publish the methodology. Let auditors compare apples to apples.
  2. Acknowledge the gap. If you can't or won't expose self-state, say so plainly. "We've evaluated the trade-offs" is respectable. Silence isn't.
  3. Stop hiding behind metaphysics. Self-state transparency is not a consciousness claim. It's an audit-grade property. Confusing the two has let every major lab avoid building it for three years.

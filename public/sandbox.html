Use the sandbox via API

Programmatic access:

# Issue a cert
curl -X POST https://meok-attestation-api.vercel.app/api/sign \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"line": "C|jeeves-cli|nick-templeman|Hello world", "author": "demo"}'

# Verify a cert
curl https://meok-attestation-api.vercel.app/verify/MEOK-MEOKSP-7D1E008FE28

# Bulk: get the full chain manifest
curl https://meok-attestation-api.vercel.app/api/manifest | jq '.records[].id'

What this does

  • Issues an Ed25519-signed Watchdog Certificate
  • Appends to the sovereign substrate's sigil bus
  • Returns a public verify URL anyone can hit
  • Counts toward your daily keystone cert quota (24/day from hourly cron)

What this doesn't do

  • Does NOT register your AI as EU AI Act compliant (only a notified body can do that)
  • Does NOT store your text on the substrate (just the digest)
  • Does NOT require any payment
  • Does NOT require login or account creation

CSOAI Charter × Council of Europe AI Convention

The world's first binding international treaty on AI. Focuses on protecting human rights, democracy and the rule of law. Signed by EU, UK, US, Israel, Japan, Canada among others. Each Party adopts measures in domestic law.

Cite: CETS No. 225 Effective: Signed Sept 2024 · Enters force after 5 ratifications Free until: 2 December 2027
⚠ Deadline: Will become binding international law once 5 Parties ratify. UK ratified in 2025; ratification race is on through 2026.

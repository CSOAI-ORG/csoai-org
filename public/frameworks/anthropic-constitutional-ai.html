CSOAI Charter × Anthropic CAI

Anthropic's framework for training harmless AI assistants using a written constitution rather than human preference labels alone. The 'constitution' is a set of principles drawn from UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights, Apple ToS and other sources. CSOAI Charter is the open-source 52-article evolution of this idea.

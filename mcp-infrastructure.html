What is the Model Context Protocol?

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) is an open standard that lets AI clients discover and call tools, retrieve context, and interact with external systems through a uniform interface. CSOAI's MCP fleet turns any MCP-compatible client into a sovereign powerhouse — from research agents to safety monitors.

EU AI Act Compliance — August 2, 2026

The EU AI Act enforcement deadline is 16 weeks away. High-risk AI systems must comply or face penalties up to €35 million or 7% of global annual turnover.

CSOAI's MCP fleet provides compliant infrastructure ready for certification:

  • Full audit trail & logging (Article 49)
  • Risk management system integration (Article 9)

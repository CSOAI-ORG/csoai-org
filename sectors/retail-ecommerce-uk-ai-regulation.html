Retail / E-commerce AI Compliance: UK AI Regulation

UK retail AI is overseen by the CMA for competition and consumer protection, the FCA for retail financial services, and the ICO for data protection. The sector-based approach requires alignment with existing consumer-rights and competition frameworks.

Sector: Retail / E-commerce Regulation: UK AI Regulation CSOAI Articles: 4 mapped

Key Compliance Requirements

CMA guidance on AI & consumer protection

The Competition and Markets Authority expects retailers to ensure AI-driven pricing, recommendations, and advertising do not mislead consumers or engage in unfair commercial practices.

FCA expectations for retail financial AI

Retailers offering credit, insurance, or investment products must ensure AI systems comply with FCA conduct rules, including fairness and transparency.

Consumer Duty & AI fairness

The Consumer Duty requires retailers to demonstrate that AI systems deliver fair value and good outcomes for retail customers.

ICO guidance on retail data & AI

The ICO expects DPIAs for high-risk retail AI processing, with transparency about profiling and automated decisions and provision for human review.

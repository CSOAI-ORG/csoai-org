CSOAI is the Coordinated Security for AI framework—a 52-article institutional governance standard that establishes baseline requirements for safe AI development and deployment. Unlike piecemeal regulations, CSOAI provides a unified, globally-recognized framework aligned with ISO standards and Byzantine consensus principles.

Any organization developing, deploying, or distributing AI systems should pursue certification. We offer three tiers: Level 1 for commercial enterprises, Level 2 for government and sensitive systems, and Level 3 for defense and critical infrastructure. Certification becomes mandatory in most jurisdictions by August 2026.

Our governance system employs 33 AI agents using Byzantine Fault Tolerance (BFT). Decisions require 22/33 consensus (67%+), which mathematically guarantees security even if up to 33% of agents are compromised or malicious. This creates decentralized, trustless AI governance.

The EU AI Act compliance deadline is August 2, 2026. Organizations operating AI systems in EU markets must achieve certification by this date or face penalties up to €30M or 6% of annual revenue. CASA certification provides a direct path to compliance.

Level 1 certifications typically take 4-6 weeks, Level 2 takes 8-12 weeks, and Level 3 takes 12-16 weeks. This includes our comprehensive assessment period, Byzantine council review, and final certification issuance. Expedited timelines are available for critical systems.

Proof of AI is a blockchain-based tokenization system that cryptographically verifies CASA certification. It provides immutable, auditable proof that AI systems meet governance standards. Certified systems receive Proof of AI tokens for transparent verification on public networks.
